neta bomani ~ 100 days of zines by neta bomani

neta bomani is an abolitionist, educator and zine maker who is interested in parsing information and histories while making things by hand with human and non-human computers.

This website is an archived zine project from January 28 - May 7, 2021, where Neta made and posted a zine made on the same day, everyday for 100 consecutive days as an extension of the 100 Days of Making class in the Interactive Telecommunications program at NYU Tisch School of the Arts. The 100 days ended in a final complicated zine called Dark Matter Objects which doubled as Neta's graduate thesis.
