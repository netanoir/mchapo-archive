neta bomani ~ mchapo archive by neta bomani

neta bomani is an abolitionist, educator and zine maker who is interested in parsing information and histories while making things by hand with human and non-human computers.

This website is a zine archiving project from January 28 - May 7, 2021, where Neta posted a zine study and/or experiment everyday for at least 100 days as an extension of the 100 Days of Making class in the Interactive Telecommunications program at NYU Tisch School of the Arts.
