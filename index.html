neta bomani ~ mchapo archive by neta bomani

neta bomani is a worker who engages in oral history, direct action and social practices. neta's work has materialized as do-it-yourself computational objects, hand-coded programs and abolitionist gestures like organizing and making archives, writings, prints, zines, networks and workshops.

This website is a zine archiving project beginning on January 28, 2021, where Neta will post a zine study and/or experiment everyday for at least 100 days as an extension of the 100 Days of Making class in the Interactive Telecommunications program at NYU Tisch School of the Arts.
